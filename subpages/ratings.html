Pavement Types

You're seeing the breakdown of pavement type in years 2001 and 2015. The ratio of pavement types remains about the same. Around 100 roads out of just over 3,000 are full depth aspahlt with no base of any kind. Roads with 3+ inches of asphalt over a concrete base account for about 60% of roads on the data collected in both recordings.
